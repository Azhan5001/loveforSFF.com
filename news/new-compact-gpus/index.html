    When building a small form factor (SFF) PC, one of the most critical components to consider is the GPU. Compact GPUs are essential for fitting into tight spaces while still delivering excellent performance. In this article, we’ll explore the best compact GPUs for SFF builds in 2024, focusing on performance, size, and value.

    1. NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 Ti (Compact Edition)

    Key Features:

    • GPU: NVIDIA Ada Lovelace Architecture
    • VRAM: 8GB GDDR6
    • Length: 200mm
    • Power Consumption: 160W

    The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 Ti is a powerhouse in a compact form factor. With its efficient Ada Lovelace architecture, this GPU delivers excellent 1080p and 1440p gaming performance while staying cool and quiet. Its 200mm length makes it perfect for most SFF cases.

    Pros:

    • Excellent performance for its size
    • DLSS 3 support for improved frame rates
    • Low power consumption

    Cons:

    • Slightly higher price point
    • Limited to 8GB VRAM

    2. AMD Radeon RX 7600 (ITX Version)

    Key Features:

    • GPU: AMD RDNA 3 Architecture
    • VRAM: 8GB GDDR6
    • Length: 180mm
    • Power Consumption: 165W

    The AMD Radeon RX 7600 in its ITX form is a fantastic choice for budget-conscious SFF builders. It offers solid 1080p gaming performance and supports AMD’s FidelityFX Super Resolution (FSR) for enhanced visuals. At just 180mm long, it fits into even the smallest cases.

    Pros:

    • Affordable price
    • Compact design
    • Great for 1080p gaming

    Cons:

    • Limited ray tracing performance
    • No DLSS support

    3. Intel Arc A750 (Low-Profile Edition)

    Key Features:

    • GPU: Intel Xe HPG Architecture
    • VRAM: 8GB GDDR6
    • Length: 190mm
    • Power Consumption: 150W

    Intel’s Arc A750 in a low-profile design is a surprising contender in the compact GPU market. It offers excellent value for money, with performance comparable to mid-range GPUs from NVIDIA and AMD. Its 190mm length and low power consumption make it ideal for SFF builds.

    Pros:

    • Great value for money
    • Solid 1080p and 1440p performance
    • Supports Intel XeSS for upscaling

    Cons:

    • Driver support still improving
    • Limited availability

    4. NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 (Dual-Fan Compact Design)

    Key Features:

    • GPU: NVIDIA Ada Lovelace Architecture
    • VRAM: 12GB GDDR6X
    • Length: 220mm
    • Power Consumption: 200W

    For those who need more power, the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 in a dual-fan compact design is a top pick. It delivers exceptional 1440p and even 4K gaming performance, thanks to its 12GB of GDDR6X memory and DLSS 3 support. At 220mm, it’s slightly larger but still fits many SFF cases.

    Pros:

    • Excellent 1440p and 4K performance
    • DLSS 3 support
    • Future-proof with 12GB VRAM

    Cons:

    • Higher power consumption
    • Pricier than other options

    5. AMD Radeon RX 7700 XT (SFF Edition)

    Key Features:

    • GPU: AMD RDNA 3 Architecture
    • VRAM: 12GB GDDR6
    • Length: 210mm
    • Power Consumption: 190W

    The AMD Radeon RX 7700 XT in its SFF edition is a great choice for gamers who want high performance in a compact package. It excels at 1440p gaming and supports AMD’s FSR for improved visuals. Its 210mm length makes it compatible with most SFF cases.

    Pros:

    • Great 1440p performance
    • Supports FSR 2.0
    • Good value for money

    Cons:

    • Slightly larger than other compact GPUs
    • Ray tracing performance lags behind NVIDIA

    Conclusion

    Choosing the right compact GPU for your SFF build depends on your budget, performance needs, and case size. Here’s a quick summary of our top picks:

    GPU Model Best For Length Price Range
    NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 Ti High performance 200mm $400 - $500
    AMD Radeon RX 7600 Budget-friendly 180mm $300 - $350
    Intel Arc A750 Value for money 190mm $250 - $300
    NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 1440p/4K gaming 220mm $600 - $700
    AMD Radeon RX 7700 XT Balanced performance 210mm $450 - $500

    Whether you’re building a compact gaming rig or a portable workstation, these GPUs offer the perfect balance of size and performance. Happy building!
