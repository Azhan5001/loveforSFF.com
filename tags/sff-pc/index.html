      Budget SFF PC: Building a High-Performance Small Form Factor PC Without Breaking the Bank

      Building a small form factor (SFF) PC doesn’t have to drain your wallet. With smart planning and careful component choices, you can put together a compact yet high-performance system that stays within your budget. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the top budget components for an SFF build in 2025 and share tips to save money without sacrificing performance.

      Why Build a Budget SFF PC?

      Small form factor PCs are ideal for gamers, content creators, and professionals who need a powerful machine in a portable package. Although SFF builds can sometimes be expensive due to specialized parts, focusing on value-driven components lets you create a sleek, compact system without overspending.
